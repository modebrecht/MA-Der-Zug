4. pressure shift;
5. slowing;

Pass.

## 4. Figure audit

### Levin

Pass.

Levin stays controlled, observant and avoidant. He does not become a detective too early. His knowledge remains bodily and procedural, not autobiographical.

Strong choices:

- he tries to normalize sound, light and motion;
- he uses other passengers as calibration;
- `Störung`, `betrieblich` and `Sichtprüfung` affect him before he understands why;
- he resists the memory impulse at the end instead of following it.

This matches his canon: detail-observer, rationalizer, and bearer of unplaced railway knowledge.

### Marlene

Pass.

Marlene watches better than the others, but does not explain. She asks precise questions and notices Levin’s reaction, yet does not become a comfort figure.

Her camera remains present as an observation object, not a solution. This correctly saves the proper camera payoff for Chapter 4.

### Tobias Reck

Pass with watch.

Tobias takes interpretive authority exactly as planned. He is useful, irritating and socially plausible. He converts uncertainty into order-language and makes others feel that someone has a handle on things.

Watch for Chapter 3:

- He is already strong in Ch2 through `Störung`, `Abläufe`, `Freigabe`, `Sichtprüfung` and social command.
- Ch3 must now make him more concrete through seat, coffee, luggage, ticket and lived presence rather than simply adding more process-language.
- Avoid overusing `Abnahme`, `Protokoll`, `in Ordnung` before the later reveal chain needs them.

### Mancini

Pass.

Mancini is polite, brief and cold. He acknowledges the halt but gives no usable cause, no timeline and no clean next step. This is precisely his function: threshold / service / witness, not oracle.

Strong choices:

- “Es ist die Antwort, die ich Ihnen geben kann.”
- “Der Zug ist angehalten.”
- “Absurd ist kein Betriebszustand.”
- “Wenn es notwendig ist.”

These lines are heavy without becoming lore.

### Frau Hediger

Pass.

She stays too calm and slightly knowing without becoming an exposition machine. Her folding the newspaper, looking out not searching but recognizing, and lowering her eyes before Mancini create pressure without answers.

### Eliah / Selma-Anika cluster

Pass.

Eliah’s sound sensitivity and early resistance to reassurance are well seeded. The chapter does not over-explain him or use the mother-child trauma cheaply.

### Sarah

Pass.

Sarah remains an outside-world anchor through Levin’s unsent message impulse. She is not overused.
