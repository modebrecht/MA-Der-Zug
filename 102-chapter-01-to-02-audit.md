## 4. Style continuity

Chapter 2 continues the key stylistic traits from Chapter 1:

- tight Levin POV
- controlled literary perception
- bodily reactions instead of direct explanation
- short hard sentences after longer observational passages
- sensory details becoming psychologically charged
- restrained uncanny atmosphere

The strongest continuity point is that Chapter 2 does not treat the final sound from Chapter 1 as simple plot information. It treats it as inner knowledge that Levin refuses to touch.

This matches Chapter 1's core movement:

- sound
- bodily reaction
- refusal / repression

---

## 5. Transition quality

The Chapter 1 to Chapter 2 seam is strong.

Chapter 1 ends with Levin knowing, without knowing why, that he has heard the metallic splitting sound before.

Chapter 2 begins in the aftermath of that sound. It remains inside the tunnel / train pressure instead of jumping to a new scene.

The transition logic works like this:

1. metallic splitting
2. bodily aftershock
3. movement becomes uncertain
4. the train halt becomes believable and uncanny
5. the private fracture becomes a shared world-break

This is the correct escalation.

## 6. Strong elements in Chapter 2

### 6.1 The halt

The halt is handled well because it does not feel like an action scene.

It arrives through:

- pressure change
- weight
- light change
- heavier silence
- social reaction in the carriage

This fits the Albula project rules.

### 6.2 Mancini

Mancini remains effective because his surface role still feels ordinary.

His sentences stay simple and formal, but slightly wrong. This keeps him from becoming an explainer too early.

### 6.3 Escalation

Chapter 2 correctly turns Chapter 1's private unease into a shared disturbance.

Chapter 1 is mainly Levin's body and perception.

Chapter 2 begins to make the carriage react.

## 7. Watchpoints for Chapter 2 control pass

### 7.1 Sichtprüfung

The word `Sichtprüfung` is strong because it points toward the later form / signature reveal.

It works if it remains rare and unexplained.

Rule: keep it rare. Do not repeat it too often before the actual reveal.

### 7.2 02:47

The 02:47 ending is effective, but the control pass should check intensity.

If the number appears too many times, the ending may become more forceful than Chapter 1's subtler style.

Rule: check whether the ending needs every repetition, or whether fewer instances would be more elegant.

### 7.3 Mancini

Mancini should not become more pointed yet.

Rule: keep him quiet, formal and slightly wrong. Do not turn him into an explainer.

### 7.4 Reck / Tobias

Reck should stay plausible as a person, not become only a symbol of system responsibility.

Rule: let his behavior carry the role. Do not make him more obvious.
